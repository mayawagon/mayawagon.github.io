Case study

{{page.title}}

This interactive video piece, created in collaboration with Yao Tong as part of the MIT Workshop I course, combines archival blues dancing footage with contemporary clips from Youtube in order to challenge the viewer to consider what it means to appropriate cultural systems. The interpretation by the viewer is left intentionally open for dialogue.
