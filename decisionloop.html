DecisionLoop is a design for a responsive web application that addresses the usability shortcomings of the current system of public political participation. For organizers, it provides a way to gather action steps around a cause and to advertise those steps to people interested in the cause. For people who may not be familiar with political strategy, it would provide an easily accessible, jargon-free place to find out anything they can do to help.

This project was developed in two weeks at the General Assembly User Experience Design Immersive program, and later taken to Code for San Francisco, a volunteer civic technology group. The initial design process is documented below:
